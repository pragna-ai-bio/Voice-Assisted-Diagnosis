Voice Assisted Parkinson's Disease Diagnosis

Experience our innovative voice analysis technology for Parkinson's disease research. This demo leverages models trained on the UCI "Parkinson Speech Dataset with Multiple Types of Sound Recordings" to analyze speech signals and detect subtle vocal biomarkers associated with neurological conditions.

Our voice analysis focuses on detecting micro-tremors, frequency variations, and speech pattern changes that may indicate early-stage Parkinson's disease, often before visible motor symptoms appear. The system analyzes multiple acoustic parameters including:

  • Fundamental frequency variations
  • Amplitude variations
  • Harmonic-to-noise ratio
  • Vocal tremor patterns
  • Speech rate and rhythm
